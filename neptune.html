About Neptune Beach

Neptune Beach is a charming beachfront city located east of Jacksonville, Florida. Nestled between Atlantic Beach and Jacksonville Beach, it offers a relaxed atmosphere with plenty of local dining and shopping options. The area is known for its pastel-painted architecture, beautiful sandy shores, and a laid-back vibe that attracts visitors of all ages.
