Atlantic Beach Farmers Market

The mission of the Atlantic Beach Farmers Market is to promote local food and local artists. To build a community where we work and live. We support our local economy, increase market opportunities for farmers and small businesses and provide access to an assortment of local and regional sourced products. We want to create a destination, a place where the market serves as a setting for the community and visitors to the area to gather and learn something new from a farmer, grower, producer or exhibitor. The market is held every Sunday, 10 am until 2 pm Jack Russell Park ~ 800 Seminole Rd ~ Atlantic Beach FL 32233

2-Day Pottery Workshop: Fairy House

Join Chelsea at ABAM for a 2-day Pottery Workshop to create a fairy house on Friday, 4/3 & 4/17! In this workshop students will create decorative hand-built pieces using the various methods. This workshop occurs on two days, two weeks apart: Friday, April 3rd from 10am-12pm & Friday, April 17th from 9am-12pm In DAY ONE of the workshop, students will make a fairy house and various accessories, such as a bridge and/or stepping stones, with instructor assistance, as needed. Students will glaze the pieces on DAY TWO of the workshop and will get to choose from a variety of color options. Tickets: $65 per person Instructor: Chelsea Leonard
